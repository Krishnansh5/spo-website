Abhinav D Singh , studying in IIT Kanpur as a BT student in AE will be joining McKinsey & Company as Bussiness Analyst. Here are some brief insights of his.

## Shortlisted Profiles & Companies:

- BCG associate

## Insights on the Selection Process
- Resume shortlist
- Test (ask you to play games)
- Buddy interview - 3 rounds: guesstimate, 2 case rounds (case revolves around resume)

##  Preparation Resources
-Aditya Agarwal
-Victor cheng
-CIC (beginners’ level)
-Lima case book

## Advice for students (dos and don’ts)? 
- All processes are evaluative, be cautioned, be polite and do your best regardless of whether they are evaluative or not
- In case your company asks for other shortlists and preference orders, refrain from showing
your inclination or preference towards other companies, instead try reasoning out why you are a
good fit in this company rather than others.
