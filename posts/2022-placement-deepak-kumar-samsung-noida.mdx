## Insights on the Selection Process
- Resume Shortlist- CSE and MTH (Both BT and MT), 7.5+ gpa
- Test – A 3 hrs long test with 1 coding question on Multi-source BFS and had 50 test cases in which you advance to interview only if you solve all 50 test cases

##  Preparation Resources

## Advice for students (dos and don’ts)? 
- Show the HR representative that you have a genuine interest in the business by showing that
you are well-versed in its past and history.
- Avoid being too candid when responding to questions about whether you would leave the job
for a higher salary.
