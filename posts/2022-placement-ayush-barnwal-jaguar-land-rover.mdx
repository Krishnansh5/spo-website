## Advice for students (dos and don’ts)? 
- Try to develop some proficiency in algorithms and their implementation in a language of choice.
- Prepare your resume well and should have a thorough knowledge of each and every point present in your resume.
- For interviews, try to polish communication skills and temperament. You don’t need to answer every question correctly. Try to do as best as you can.
