Ayush Sakya  , studying in IIT Kanpur as a DOUBLE MAJOR student in CSE will be joining Glean as SDE. Here are some brief insights of his.

## Shortlisted Profiles & Companies:
- Graviton (Quant)
- NK Securities (Quant and SDE both)
- Sprinklr (SDE both I and II)

## Insights on the Selection Process
- Resume Shortlist:- Cse only (9.5+ GPA)
- No test no Gd
- Three Rounds of Technical Interviews of about 45 min each with one main programming question and optimizing it further.
- Other than this, round 1 also consisted of 1-word DSA questions like which STL can do x in y time complexity.

##  Preparation Resources
- Standard Leetcode, GFG and interview Bit questions
- Codeforces and Atcoders contest
- Test series from Archives by Pclub IITK

## Advice for students (dos and don’ts)? 
Do:
- Given that all of the rounds were technical rounds, communication was a differentiator
- Maintain a sturdy foundation; it will always be useful.
- Keep Giving contests on a regular basis as it helps in timing sense as well as finding approaches for new questions

Don’ts : 
- Don't respond in a hurry, Consider your response before speaking, and check with the interviewer your approaches before proceeding to code.
