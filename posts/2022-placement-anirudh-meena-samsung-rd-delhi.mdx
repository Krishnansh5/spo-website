Anirudh Meena, studying in IIT Kanpur as a BT student in EE will be joining Samsung R&D Delhi as SDE. Here are some brief insights of his.

## Shortlisted Profiles & Companies:
- Walmart (DS)
- Edge Verve (SDE)

## Insights on the Selection Process
- Online Assessment in their own portal which doesn’t allow the use of STL(standard template Libraries)
- Technical Interview
- HR Round

- Initially, they called us into groups of three students and after the introduction, they provided an easy-level DSA-based question and set a timer for five minutes to solve it.
- After that, they conducted one on one interviews.
- The interviewer discussed the projects that I had mentioned in my resume.
- In HR round we basically discussed my internships and why I chose this field and also my life at IITK.

##  Preparation Resources
- Programming Pathsala for DSA ( it was nice and well-structured)

## Advice for students (dos and don’ts)? 
- Choose your resources carefully and stick to them.
- Be well-prepared for the HR round by practising frequently asked questions to get started.
- Be confident
